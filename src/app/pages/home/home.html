Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our network and services.

  • How do I connect to the South Africa network?

    You can connect through our various Points of Presence (PoPs) located in major data centers across Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban. Contact our sales team for a custom solution.

  • What is the latency between London and South Africa?

    Our network utilizes the most direct submarine cable routes, providing industry-leading latency of approximately 140ms between London and Cape Town.

  • Do you provide 24/7 technical support?

    Yes, we operate a 24/7 Network Operations Center (NOC) that monitors our global infrastructure and provides immediate assistance to our partners and customers.
